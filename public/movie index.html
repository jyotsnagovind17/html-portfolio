The Best Movies According to Jyotsna

My top 3 movies of all time!

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

This movie is really close to my heart, and makes me smile every single time
It shows how beautiful friendship can be and also teaches us to live life to the fullest.

Taare Zameen Par

This is a movie about a dyslexic kid who finds it hard to read and write and slowly
learns to with the help and guidance of his teacher.

3 Idiots

Talks about how Engineering does not have to be every kids dream.
